Breathe life into your Brick Builder creations with Kinekt, a powerful addon that lets you effortlessly animate elements as visitors scroll through your page.

== Description ==

**Unleash the Power of Captivating On-Scroll Animations with Kinekt**

In today's digital landscape, capturing user attention and delivering an engaging experience is paramount to success. With Kinekt, the revolutionary Brick Builder addon, you can effortlessly elevate your web designs by incorporating eye-catching on-scroll animations that breathe life into your content.

**Transform Static Pages into Dynamic Experiences**

Kinekt seamlessly integrates with GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform) and ScrollTrigger, two industry-leading animation libraries, to empower you with a vast array of animation possibilities. Whether it's subtle transitions, dynamic reveals, or captivating parallax effects, Kinekt puts the power of animation at your fingertips.

**Unlock the Ease of Use with Bricks Builder**

Leveraging the intuitive interface of Bricks Builder, Kinekt eliminates the complexities of animation coding, making it accessible to designers of all skill levels. With simple drag-and-drop interactions and a user-friendly interface, you can create sophisticated animations in a matter of minutes.

**Enhance User Engagement and Boost Conversions**

Studies have shown that on-scroll animations can significantly enhance user engagement, leading to increased time spent on pages, reduced bounce rates, and improved conversion rates. Kinekt empowers you to create compelling animations that guide visitors through your content, keeping them engaged and driving desired actions.

**Kinekt: The Key to Unlocking Next-Level Web Design**

With Kinekt, you can:

* Transform static web pages into dynamic experiences
* Create captivating on-scroll animations with ease
* Leverage the power of GSAP and ScrollTrigger libraries
* Enhance user engagement and boost conversions

Embrace the future of web design and elevate your creations with Kinekt.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `ooohboi-kinekt.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions == 

= Does this plugin include the GSAP and/or ScrollTrigger libraries? =

Not physically! These libraries are loaded from the public (CDN) repository: https://cdnjs.com/libraries/gsap/latest 

= Which other 3rd party libraries are used by OoohBoi Kinekt? = 

"SplitType" by Luke Peavey. This library is also loaded from the global content delivery network; https://unpkg.com/split-type
